My Top 9 Films

The International edition

I love watching movies, good movies. High IMDB and Rotten Tomatoes rating good movies. Although I'll indulge in cheesy animated movies for kids (strictly Pixar and Studio Ghibli with a little bit of Disney, but only the really good ones like Big Hero 6, The Lion King, The jungle book you get what I mean). And a fair mix off horrible but nostalgic guilty pleasure movies.

Some have been mind-boggling woah moments, others have left me with the ick. Mind most of these have been American or English productions which is pretty limiting and basic of me.

But a whole new world was unlocked once I discovered the world of international films. Big hits or smaller scale productions, most of them never seem to miss.

So I decided to show international films the love they deserve. This is a compilation of 9 international films from 9 countries that left me crying and smiling like a fool that I think everyone NEEDS to watch.

Some of you will find certain watches a waste of your time, some might just discover a new favourite, some may be hit in the face with nostalgia, most will not even think twice about giving these a watch.

But for the ones who do, I hope this website in some way can be the place that sparks an interest and gives you enough to consider these films worth your while.



With that said, I am delighted to officially invite you to,
