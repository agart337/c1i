Year: 2001

Country/ Countries - Japan

Language: Japanese

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Starring: Rumi Hiiragi, Miyu Irino, Mari Natsuki, Bunta Sugawara, Yumi Tamai

Music by: Composed by Joe Hisaishi and performed by the New Japan Philharmonic symphony orchestra

Synopsis: The story follows a 10-year-old girl named Chihiro, who, while moving to a new town, stumbles into a mysterious and magical world ruled by spirits, witches, and strange creatures. Her parents are transformed into pigs, and Chihiro must navigate this strange spirit realm to find a way to break the spell and save them. The film revolves around her journey as she navigates her way into adulthood, taking responsibility for herself and her parents.
